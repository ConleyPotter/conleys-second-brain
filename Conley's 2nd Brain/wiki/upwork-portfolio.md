## Overview

Four portfolio projects are documented for the ACE Phase I Upwork profile. Projects 1 is ready to execute this week; Projects 2–4 require pulling data from work AI assistants.

**Upwork character limits:**
- Project title: 70 characters max
- Your role: 100 characters max
- Project description: 600 characters max
- Skills and deliverables: 5 max

**The spec project unlocks everything.** It's the most credible piece because it's real output for the exact service being sold. Do it first.

## Portfolio Upload Guidelines

| Project Type | Best Upload Format |
|---|---|
| Lead list projects | Screenshot of CSV — columns visible, email column blurred |
| CRM enrichment | Before/after comparison — field completeness, two columns |
| Market research | One-page PDF: research scope, methodology, output (no proprietary data) |
| ABM campaign | Visual targeting criteria pyramid: Industry → Company Size → Title |

All assets: clean and minimal. No logos, no client names, no identifiable branding.
