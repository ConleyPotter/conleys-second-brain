# Wedding Prayer

**Related:** [[conley-potter]], [[domain-personal]]

---

## Overview

Two prayers written for Conley and Sami's wedding on June 12, 2026. The first is a ceremony prayer delivered by their officiant, Olivia. The second is a dinner speech and prayer written and delivered by Conley himself.

---

## Ceremony prayer (by Olivia, officiant)

Olivia's prayer opens the ceremony by acknowledging the weight and joy of the moment — asking for presence, patience, and grace over the marriage. The prayer names Conley and Sami directly, grounding it in their specific relationship rather than generic liturgy.

Key themes:
- Asking that the marriage be rooted in patience, kindness, and forgiveness
- Recognition that love is built daily through small, consistent choices
- A blessing over the home they will share — that it be a place of peace, laughter, and honesty
- Gratitude for the community witnessing the vows

---

## Dinner speech and prayer (by Conley)

Conley's speech doubles as a toast and a prayer — he addresses the gathered friends and family before shifting into a direct expression of gratitude. The tone is personal, grounded, and emotionally direct.

Key themes:
- Gratitude to Sami for her steadiness and for choosing to build a life together
- Acknowledgement of the people in the room — specifically those who shaped Conley and Sami's relationship
- A prayer over the meal and the evening — asking for joy, presence, and memory-making
- A closing that frames the wedding not as an arrival but as a beginning
