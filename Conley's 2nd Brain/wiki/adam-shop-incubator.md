# Adam's Auto Repair Shop Incubator

**Related:** [[ba-overview]], [[ba-team]], [[conley-potter]]

---

## What It Is

Adam Kushner (BA founder) has purchased an auto repair shop — the first location in a planned chain of up to **6 auto repair shops**. This is not a BA agency project; it's Adam's personal investment and strategic initiative. However, it intersects with BA's work in two ways:

1. **Content engine** — the shops serve as a live marketing incubator for BA's own campaigns and creative work
2. **Real estate hold** — long-term property investment play for Adam

The shops will double as proving grounds for BA's digital marketing services — meaning BA's own methodologies can be tested, documented, and refined on a live business Adam controls, rather than a client's.

## Strategic Rationale

**For Adam:**
- **Content engine:** Live shops provide authentic content material for BA's marketing case studies, video production (already a BA service), and build-in-public documentation
- **Incubator:** Testing new BA services on owned properties de-risks client pitches — BA can demonstrate what works before selling it
- **Real estate:** Physical property held long-term. Auto repair shops in the right markets can be acquired and operated with meaningful cash flow

**For Conley:**
- Jim Walton referral deepens Conley's relationship with Adam outside the formal employer/employee context
- The project may create future opportunities: content about the shops could appear on Building Out Loud, Conley's systems work could apply to shop operations, or the project could become relevant to ACE's Phase II or III (RevOps for physical service businesses)
