# Mom's Housing Offer — Multi-Gen Property in Lebanon, PA

## The offer

Conley's mom and her partner Jim have invited Conley and Sami to move into a **six-bedroom multi-generation property** at **1403 Douglas Fir Drive, Lebanon, PA** (Cornwall School District). The arrangement would give each household a fully independent living space within the same house.

## Property layout

- **Upper floor (Mom & Jim):** 4 bedrooms, kitchen, living room, multiple bathrooms
- **Lower floor (Conley & Sami):** 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, kitchen, living room — approximately **2,000 sq ft**
- The lower level is **not a basement** — the house is built on a hill, providing abundant natural light and windows
- **Master bedroom:** 8 feet of nearly floor-to-ceiling windows, private patio, separate entryway, and own driveway
- **Master bath:** Whirlpool tub plus separate shower
- **Outdoor:** Large garage, exterior building (workshop or art studio potential), 2.5–3 acres with private hiking trails

## Timeline and terms

- **Closing:** June 30, 2026
- **Renovations before move-in:** Seal off connecting staircase between floors, replace carpet with LVP downstairs, bathroom updates (new vanities at minimum)
- **Rent:** ~$1,100/month ($1,000–1,200 range), **all utilities included**

## Purpose and family context

Mom's motivation is to help Conley and Sami in three ways:

1. **Enable Sami to stop working** — creating space for starting a family
2. **Provide childcare support** — Mom wants to help watch the baby as much as Conley and Sami are comfortable with
3. **Accelerate savings** — reduced housing costs to save for a first home purchase
