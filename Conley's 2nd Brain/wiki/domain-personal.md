## What this domain covers

The Personal domain captures Conley's inner life, operating philosophy, physical and relational infrastructure, long-range goals, and the projects that exist outside or alongside ACE. Unlike the ACE domain — which is about building a system — this domain is about the person running the system.

Pages here address:
- Annual operating doctrine and year themes
- Decision frameworks and principles
- Body, capacity, and health (fibromyalgia, training, sustainability)
- Relationships and home
- Inner work, meaning, and spiritual practice
- Paused or long-range personal projects

## Current year at a glance (2026)

**Theme:** Consolidation and Signal

**Primary objective:** Financial security with an expansive arc — calm cash flow, no background financial anxiety, decisions made from stability not urgency.

**End conditions:**
- Income feels calm and expandable
- ACE is a coherent, living system
- *Building Out Loud* is a clear and trustworthy public signal
- Body and mind feel steady under load
- Life feels *larger*, not louder
