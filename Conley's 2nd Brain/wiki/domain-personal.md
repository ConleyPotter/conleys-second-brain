## What this domain covers

The Personal domain captures Conley's inner life, operating philosophy, physical and relational infrastructure, long-range goals, and the projects that exist outside or alongside his professional work. Unlike the professional domains — which are about building systems — this domain is about the person running the systems.

Pages here address:
- Identity, career history, and public presence
- Annual operating doctrine and year themes
- Decision frameworks and principles
- Body, capacity, and health (fibromyalgia, training, sustainability)
- Relationships and home
- Inner work, meaning, and spiritual practice
- Resume, CV, and career transition strategy
- Portfolio and professional presentation
- Paused or long-range personal projects
