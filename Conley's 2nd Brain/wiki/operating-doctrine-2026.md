# 2026 Operating Doctrine

**Related:** [[conley-potter]], [[domain-personal]], [[building-out-loud]], [[ace-overview]], [[brand-voice]]

---

## Year Theme

**Consolidation and Signal**

2026 is not a year of expansion by addition. It is a year of expansion by *clarity, leverage, and compounding focus*.

---

## 1. Primary Objective (Non-Negotiable)

**Financial security with an expansive arc.**

- Calm, predictable cash flow
- No background financial anxiety
- Decisions made from stability, not urgency

Stability is not the goal. Stability is the *platform*.

---

## 2. Core Leverage Stack

### A. Income Foundation — Business Actualization (BA)

- Primary, compounding income engine
- Role expanding in scope, responsibility, and commission
- No plan to exit in 2026 (or likely 2027)
- Professional alignment and reputation are protected at all times

All public work must remain clearly non-competitive with BA.

### B. Singular Owned Asset — Personal Brand Engine (PBE)

- The successor to ACE and TLE, merged into a unified system in May 2026
- Purpose shifted from freelance revenue generation to career capital generation: public presence, open source portfolio projects, and content authority that powers the career transition
- Success measured by: LinkedIn/GitHub/Substack growth, portfolio projects shipped, inbound recruiter quality, and ultimately landing the target role
- See [[personal-brand-engine]] for full architecture

**Explicitly paused** (2025 projects on hold): [[muse]], [[companion]], [[book-project-2025]]

---

## 3. Creative Output Stance

Creativity is **secondary but present**.

Funneled into:
- Software engineering
- Data modeling
- Prompt and system design
- Generative process improvement

No obligation to ship traditional creative artifacts. If creativity ships, it must support or reflect ACE.

*Creative work exists to sharpen the machine, not distract from it.*

---

## 4. Public Presence Doctrine

### Public Persona

**Independent systems builder documenting real work.**

- Authority is allowed; authority is never sold
- No services, coaching, consulting, or funnels
- No positioning as an agency alternative
- Stance: observational, reflective, experiential

> "I'm building this. You're welcome to watch."

### Public Output

**Multi-channel. Speaking on par with written. Automated where possible.**

- LinkedIn, X, Substack, and portfolio blog are all active channels — not derivatives of each other
- Audio/speaking is a primary channel, on par with writing — format TBD (see [[building-out-loud]])
- The Thought Leader Engine (see [[thought-leader-engine]]) processes raw captures into platform-adapted drafts, making consistent multi-channel output achievable within the available bandwidth constraint
- See [[content-strategy]] for channel breakdown and cadence

---

## 5. Body, Nervous System, and Capacity

The body is infrastructure.

- Weight loss and mobility gains maintained
- Training prioritizes durability and regulation, not aesthetics
- Systems assume flare-ups and variability (fibromyalgia context — see [[conley-potter]])
- Success includes liking the body you live in

*Burnout invalidates the year.*

---

## 6. Relationships and Home

2026 is a **marriage year**, not a side quest.

- Decisions support shared rhythm and long-term container building
- Personal ambition does not override relational stability
- Long-term home vision is actively advanced

No "successful" year that costs the relationship is acceptable.

---

## 7. Inner Work and Meaning

Depth over novelty.

- One spiritual or reflective discipline that actually stays
- More lived presence
- Less narrating the self, more inhabiting it

*Integration beats identity performance.*

---

## 8. Decision Filter

Use this weekly.

**Say yes only if the thing:**
- Strengthens BA or ACE
- Increases calm, leverage, or clarity
- Compounds rather than fragments attention

**Say no if the thing:**
- Creates urgency without leverage
- Pulls energy into paused projects
- Turns authority into a commodity

When unsure: default to *restraint*.

---

## 9. End Conditions for 2026

By the end of 2026:

- Income feels calm and expandable
- ACE is a coherent, living system
- Public signal is clear and trustworthy through *Building Out Loud*
- Body and mind feel steady under load
- Life feels *larger*, not louder
