**Related:** [[ba-overview]], [[ba-products]], [[ba-partners]]

**This is a rolling page.** It documents key client relationships and pipeline accounts that appear repeatedly in Conley's recaps. HubSpot is the system of record for deal stages and exact amounts — this page captures the strategic and relational context that HubSpot deal cards don't hold.

Update this page in place as accounts evolve. Do not create a new page per client — log entries in [[log]] are for that.

## How to Read This Page

**Relationship types:**
- **Active client** — currently receiving BA services, paying monthly
- **New business** — in proposal or negotiation stage
- **AutoVitals co-managed** — BA + AutoVitals joint account; coordinated via the AV partner team
- **Prospect** — in pipeline but no proposal yet
- **Onboarding** — signed, not yet live

Accounts are organized by how recently they appeared in Conley's recaps and their strategic weight.
