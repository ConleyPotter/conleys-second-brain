## What this domain covers

The General Knowledge domain captures external articles, threads, podcast notes, and research worth retaining. Unlike the ACE and BA domains — which document Conley's own systems and work — this domain is about the broader context those systems inhabit: AI tooling, platform dynamics, industry signals, and ideas worth cross-referencing over time.

Pages here address:
- AI tool capabilities and workflow practices
- Platform and market analysis from external sources
- Research and reading notes
- Frameworks and mental models from outside sources
