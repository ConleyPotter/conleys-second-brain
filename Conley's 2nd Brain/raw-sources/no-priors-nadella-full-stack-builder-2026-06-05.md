## Episode Summary

Special crossover episode recorded at Microsoft Build where three hosts interview Satya Nadella about AI's impact on platforms, software development, and the broader tech ecosystem.

## Key Topics Discussed

1. **Microsoft Build reflections** and the latest breakthroughs announced there
2. **Multi-model harnesses** as a strategic shift for Microsoft — moving beyond single-model dependence
3. **Private evaluations (evals) as a company's "most important intellectual property"** — evals determine which models to use and how to deploy them
4. **Autonomous AI agents** and how they are transforming the software engineering role
5. **SaaS business model durability** in an AI-driven world — what survives, what gets disrupted
6. **The ROI of data centers** for local communities and why communicating that matters
7. **The token economy** and its economic and societal implications
8. **AI-driven education startups** and the future of learning
9. The concept of enabling everyone to "operate at the frontier" with frontier intelligence
10. **Vendor vs. enterprise agents** and their future trajectories
11. **Near-term model pricing predictions**
12. How Microsoft can be more ambitious going forward

## Central Thesis

The episode frames the discussion around the emergence of "full-stack builders" and "hyper-leveraged generalists" — individuals empowered by AI to do dramatically more across the technology stack. Nadella discusses how AI is collapsing the distance between having an idea and shipping a product, enabling a single person or small team to operate at a scale previously requiring large organizations.
