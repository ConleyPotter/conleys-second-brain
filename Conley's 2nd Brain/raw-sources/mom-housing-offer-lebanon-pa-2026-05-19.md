My mom invited us to move in with her and Jim into their new house in Lebanon, Cornwall. It's a six-bedroom, multi-generation property with full living spaces on the first and second floors. My mom and Jim would live on the top floor, where there are four bedrooms, a kitchen, a living room, several bathrooms, and we would live on the bottom floor, where there is a kitchen, a living room, two bedrooms, and two bathrooms. The space that we would live in is 2,000 square feet. My mom and Jim plan to close on the house on June 30, then begin the move-in process. They will do a little bit of work on the house to begin with by sealing off the stairs that connect the two levels between the first floor and the second floor. Living space on the first floor is not technically a basement because the house is built on a hill, so it has a lot of light and windows. When they do the work on the house to seal off the set of stairs, they also plan to rip up some carpet downstairs and install LPV. They also plan to redo the bathrooms or at least install new vanities. The bathroom connected to the master bedroom downstairs has a large whirlpool tub in addition to a shower. The master bedroom downstairs has eight feet of windows, nearly floor to ceiling. It also has its own patio space, its own entryway, and its own driveway, which would provide us with quite a lot of privacy. There will also be a large garage in addition to an exterior building that could house either a workshop or an art studio. The property is nearly somewhere between two and a half and three acres and has private hiking trails. It's 1,403 Douglas Fir Drive in Lebanon, PA, Cornwall School District. Rent would be $1,100, somewhere between $1,000 and $1,200, and that would include all utilities. My mom wants to do this to help Sami stop working so that she can have a baby and stay home with our kids, and we can save a bunch of money for our first house. As such, my mom also wants to help watch the baby or help to provide child care as much as we're willing to let her.
