## General Notes on Portfolio Asset Uploads

For each project, the "Add content" area in the Upwork form accepts images, files, links, and text. Recommendations by project type:

- **Lead list projects:** Upload a screenshot of the CSV (columns visible, email column blurred). Show the structure and completeness.
- **CRM enrichment:** A before/after comparison works well — two columns showing field completeness before and after. Can be a simple image created in Google Sheets.
- **Market research:** A one-page PDF summary of the research scope, methodology, and output (no proprietary data).
- **ABM campaign:** A visual showing the targeting criteria pyramid — industry > company size > title — as a simple diagram.

All images should be clean and minimal. No logos, no client names, no branding that could identify a company.
